FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about M7al licenses, features, and setup.

What is M7al POS?
M7al is a premium offline-first Point of Sale application designed for small and medium businesses to manage inventory, track sales, debts, expenses, and generate real-time reports easily.
Does it require an internet connection to run?
No, M7al works completely offline. All sales transactions, inventory records, and stats are processed locally on your device. Internet is only required for optional cloud backup synchronization.
How is my database secured?
Your database is saved directly on your local device. It is encrypted, ensuring complete privacy. No third parties or servers have access to your sensitive data.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes, depending on your license type. The Standard plan supports 1 device, while the Professional plan allows synchronization and linking across up to 3 devices in the local network.
How does the software activation work?
When you install the app, it generates a unique Device ID. You share this ID with us via WhatsApp or email to purchase a lifetime key. Paste that key in the Activation section to unlock the full app.
Are there any monthly subscriptions?
No. Standard and Professional plans are one-time lifetime purchases. You pay once and enjoy lifetime access, updates, and offline usage with no recurring fees.
Can I print receipts using barcode/thermal printers?
Yes, M7al supports all standard USB, Bluetooth, and network thermal printers (58mm and 80mm sizes) and prints customizable receipts and barcodes.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide email support for all users, and premium 24/7 WhatsApp/call support for Professional license holders to assist with database backup, installation, and general queries.
